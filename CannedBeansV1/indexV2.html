Do you know what Canned Beans CAN DO for
the prevention and management of diabetes?
 

Here are out top ten reasons why your clients should eat
more canned beans:

 
1.  Eating beans may help with the prevention of diabetes
and the manage ment of blood glucose levels.1  
2.  Eating beans can help manage blood glucose/improve
glycemic control by improving insulin sensitivity and
lowering A1c. 2  
3.  Bioactive compounds (triacylglycerolsorTAGs) in red
kidney beans show antidiabetic effects via inhibition
of α-glucosidase. 3 
4.  A recent study in Chinese T2DM patients showed
that bean-based breakfasts significantly reduced
postprandial glucose levels & promoted insulin
secretion vs. white rice-based meal. 4 
5.  Eating beans can help people lose or maintain weight. 5 
6.  Eating beans can promote digestive health.6  
7.  Adequate bean consumption can lower both total and
LDL-cholesterol levels. 7  
8.  Rinsing canned beans is an effective way to reduce total
sodium content and improve their health profile. 8  
9.  People who eat beans regularly have a longer life
expectancy than people who don’t eat beans regularly. 9  
10.  The USDA classifies beans as a subgroup of the
vegetable group and as part of the protein foods group,
so they deliver two times the nutritional punch.10

Did you ever wonder
if nutrition professionals
really think or eat
differently than most
people? We have. So, we
conducted research in
2020 and again this year
to learn more.
Not surprisingly, Registered Dietitian Nutritionists (RDNs)
and consumers vary significantly in their consumption of canned
beans and their level of understanding of the health benefits of
canned beans. We learned there are some wide knowledge gaps,
with consumers lagging far behind RDNs in understanding just
what canned beans can do.
Most RDNs know these facts, but far fewer consumers do.
