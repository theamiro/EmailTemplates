Canned Beans Are Good
For Your Heart!

You can whole-heartedly recommend canned beans for your clients—health and nutrition experts agree that beans are an important part of a healthy, nutritious and delicious diet. They are not only good for the heart, but for the whole body. With so many recipes and creative ideas on cannedbeans.org, it’s easy to add beans every day!

Check out our top ten reasons (in addition to delicious taste!) why everyone should eat more beans:
  1. Eating beans can promote digestive health.1
  2. Adequate bean consumption can lower both total and LDL-cholesterol levels.2
  3. Eating beans can help prevent heart disease. 2
  4. Eating beans can help control blood sugar. 3,4
  5. Eating beans can help people lose or maintain weight. 5
  6. Rinsing canned beans is an effective way to reduce total sodium content and improve their health profile.6
  7. People who eat beans regularly have a longer life expectancy than people who don’t eat beans regularly. 7
  8. According to research conducted by Bush Brothers and Company (March 2022), Registered Dietitian Nutritionists agree that consumers should consume more canned beans for good health. 8
  9. The USDA classifies beans as a subgroup of the vegetable group and as part of the protein foods group, so they deliver two times the nutritional punch. 9
  10. They are so versatile they can be enjoyed whole, pureed, mashed, added into other foods, or ground into flour.

Did you ever wonder if nutrition professionals really think or eat differently than most people? We have. So, we conducted research8 in 2020 and again this year to learn more.

Not surprisingly, Registered Dietitian Nutritionists (RDNs) and consumers vary significantly in their consumption of canned beans and their level of understanding of the health benefits of canned beans. We learned there are some wide knowledge gaps, with consumers lagging far behind RDNs in understanding just what canned beans can do.

Most RDNs know these facts, but far fewer consumers do. Help them understand what Canned Beans Can Do!
